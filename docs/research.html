03 — Research

Human-centered, trustworthy AI.

My research connects strong models with the people who rely on them — accurate, but also clear, safe and accountable, across adaptive interfaces, health and edge settings.

  • Trustworthy & Explainable AI
  • Human–AI Interaction

Ongoing research
  • Trustworthy & explainable skin-lesion classification. Extending a hybrid DINO-ViT + quantum-annealing optimisation pipeline on HAM10000 toward a clinician-in-the-loop tool with uncertainty quantification, visual explanations and fairness across skin tones.
  • Human-centered evaluation of vision-language models. When and why users over- or under-trust multimodal LLM outputs — and interface & calibration strategies for dependable, accessibility-focused decision support.
  • Air quality & airborne-disease burden prediction. Modeling the link between air pollution and airborne disease to forecast burden with ML over sensor and clinical data.
  • Technical vs non-technical users of LLMs. How expertise shapes prompting behaviour, interaction patterns and outcomes across conversational assistants.
